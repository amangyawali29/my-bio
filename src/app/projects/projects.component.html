Boeing Utility Freighter Aircraft Design Project
  • Worked in a team of seven to design a purpose to built freighter aircraft that can service small airports in outlying areas and can carry main deck ULDs/pallets used on large freighters.
  • Used ABACUS to perform FEM structural analysis to meet the requirement of aircraft sizing.
  • Worked in a team of seven to assemble an RC Carbon Cub and prepare a flight test to collect data and compare it with CFD results.
  • Use of ANSYS for computational fluid dynamics analysis of the RC airplane.
  • Prepare a wind tunnel test plan to collect the data according to our plan.
  • Worked in a team of four to design and fabricate a series of fluidic diode injectors and print them in 3D.
  • Designed diode in Solid works optimizing the geometry for different patterns.
  • Completed testing, calculated the diodicity of each diode and compared with Tesla valve and produced a technical report.
